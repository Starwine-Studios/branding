### RTF Extension - is a resource pack that improves and expands Russian translations for Minecraft mods on versions 1.16.5 to 1.21+

It replace the original RTF project, which is no longer maintained.
## Note
All new translations are created and tested on Minecraft 1.20.1 and above. Some translations may not work correctly on older versions.
## Contact Us
Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/BZHgF65Xp6) community if you:

- Encounter issues with RTF_E
- Want us to translate a specific mod
- Wish to contribute to our translation efforts
## Using RTF_E in Modpacks
RTF_E is designed to make modpack creation easier. You're welcome to include RTF_E in your modpack, but please credit this project
